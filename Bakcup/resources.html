Resources

These applications and tutorials can aid your process on making your project!

Sketchup - Is a simpler 3D model piece of software that can help design and conceptualize more standard and simpler products.
> Official website <
> Youtube tutorial <
Canva - This is an easy-to-use free service to create good-looking posters and information pages useful to portray your products and info.
>  Official website <
Blender - This is an advanced 3D rendering/modelling software that can help visualize more advanced and intricate products. (Not for beginners)
> Official website <
> Tutorials <
Github - This is a service/website where you can upload your code and advanced scripts, if you are developing a game/website or such for your product.
(Not for beginners)
> Official website <
Prezi
An online service that provides a tools to make a interactive presentation to portray your work/product.
> Official website <
Keynote
 A application in apple devices that provide tools to make a great looking presentation for your submission.
Powerpoint 
A application in windows and apple devices that provides tools to make presentations for your product/work.
Imovie
A free video editing software that allows you to mesh videos and record audio with inbuilt themes and effects to aid the process of creating a film/video to show your work.
